# 3) Value Pillars (what we promise) / 价值支柱（承诺什么）

## Pillar A — Carbon-neutral option & sustainability / 碳中和选项与可持续
**EN:**
- Offer project-based carbon-neutral options with a **full life-cycle** framing (production → ocean freight → local freight → end-of-life crush/reuse as road base).
- Sustainability is not decoration; it needs scope clarity, method, and proof.
> **中文：**  
> - 提供“项目制碳中和”选项，并以**全生命周期**叙事（生产→海运→本地运输→末端破碎再利用作路基）。  
> - 可持续不是装饰语，必须讲清范围、方法与证据。

## Pillar B — Predictable outcomes via shop drawings + pre-assembly / 通过图纸与预装配保证结果可预期
**EN:**
- “You see what you will get before delivery.” Reduce onsite surprises (uneven surface, cracking, color variation, aggregate inconsistency, uncertain hole amounts).
- Turn complex details (curves, LED channels, conduit drilling) into factory-controlled steps.
> **中文：**  
> - “交付前你就能看到最终结果”，减少现场不确定（不平、开裂、色差、骨料暴露不一致、孔位不确定等）。  
> - 把曲线、灯槽、穿线孔等复杂细节变成工厂可控步骤。

## Pillar C — Streamlined installation (time is money) / 安装更轻更快（时间就是成本）
**EN:**
- Replace in-situ concrete’s 7-step, 8–10 week cycle with a 3-step workflow: `Off-site pre-assembly → Delivery → Sling & Place` (typically 2–3 weeks).
- Aim: less site occupancy, fewer disruptions, fewer errors.
> **中文：**  
> - 用 3 步流程替代现浇混凝土 7 步、8–10 周周期：`预装配→送达→吊装就位`（通常 2–3 周）。  
> - 目标：更少占场、更少扰民、更少错误。

## Pillar D — Total cost control / 全成本可控
**EN:**
- Talk about **total cost** (material + labor + retouching + maintenance + risk), not unit price.
- Claims like “30–40% cheaper” must be positioned as *general circumstances* and backed with project assumptions.
> **中文：**  
> - 讲“全成本”（材料+人工+返修+维护+风险），不只比单价。  
> - “便宜 30–40%”只能作为“常规情境”口径，必须注明假设条件并可追溯。

## Pillar E — Design collaboration (WE BUILD. YOU DESIGN.) / 设计协作（你设计·我建造）
**EN:**
- Support designers from sketch to specification; protect design intent with real-world constraints and options.
- Provide samples fast; help with unusual colors/finishes; offer documentation support.
> **中文：**  
> - 从手绘到规范，帮助设计落地；在真实约束下守住设计意图。  
> - 快速样品，支持特殊颜色/表面；提供文档与技术支持。

## Pillar F — Social responsibility & ethics / 社会责任与伦理
**EN:**
- Be ready to answer: ethical sourcing, labor concerns, and “what happens to quarries after use”.
- Sustainability needs to include responsibility, not just carbon math.
> **中文：**  
> - 必须能回答：伦理采购、劳工议题、采石场开采后的修复与去向。  
> - 可持续不仅是碳核算，还包含责任链路。

# 4) Audience & segmented messaging / 受众与分层沟通

## 4.1 Primary audiences / 核心受众
**EN:** Landscape architects/designers; contractors/builders; local government/councils; community clients; developers/asset owners.  
> **中文：** 景观设计师/事务所；承包商/施工方；政府/市政；社区类客户；开发商/资产方。

## 4.2 “We speak your language” mapping / “我们懂你的语言”对照表

### Designers / 设计师
**EN focus:** Possibilities + limits, finishes, detailing, visual intent → engineered delivery.  
**Proof assets:** finish library, case studies, shop drawing samples, prototyping, 3D/QR models.  
> **中文关注点：** 可能性与边界、表面工艺、节点细节、视觉意图如何被工程化交付。  
> **证据资产：** 表面库、案例、深化图示例、打样、3D/二维码模型。

### Contractors / 承包商
**EN focus:** Predictability, speed, reduced onsite risk, clear installation guide, clamp/tool hire, logistics.  
**Proof assets:** step-by-step installation guide, pre-assembly photos, tolerances, handling guides, certified engineering study (available upon request).  
> **中文关注点：** 可预期、工期、风险更少、安装指南清晰、工具租赁、物流。  
> **证据资产：** 安装指南、预装配照片、容差与搬运指南、工程认证/计算书（可按需提供）。

### Local government / 市政与政府
**EN focus:** Social responsibility + sustainability, modular products, accessibility, reduced disruption, community fit.  
**Proof assets:** carbon-neutral scope statement, DDA-compliant examples, lifecycle maintenance.  
> **中文关注点：** 社会责任与可持续、模块化产品、无障碍、减少扰民、社区气质匹配。  
> **证据资产：** 碳中和范围说明、DDA 合规案例、全生命周期维护方案。

### Community / 社区与公共空间
**EN focus:** Safety, accessibility, comfort, durability, low maintenance, “made for people”.  
> **中文关注点：** 安全、无障碍、舒适、耐久、低维护、“以人为本”。

# 5) Voice & copy rules / 语气与文案规则

## 5.1 Voice principles / 语气原则
**EN:**
- **Design-forward, not design-only.** Every poetic line must land in a buildable proof point.
- **Bold headlines, grounded bodies.** You can be confident in titles, but always provide the “how”.
- **Structured writing wins.** Lead with outcome, then 3–5 bullets: method, proof, CTA.
> **中文：**  
> - **设计优先，但不止设计。** 每一句“美学表达”都要落到可施工的证据点。  
> - **标题可以大胆，正文必须落地。** 自信可以写在标题里，但必须给出“怎么做到”。  
> - **结构化写作。** 先给结果，再用 3–5 点讲方法/证据/下一步。

## 5.2 Newsletter style (what “good” looks like) / Newsletter 参考结构（什么叫写得好）
**EN pattern (repeatable):**
1) Big idea headline (“Power of Simplicity”, “Precision in the Dark”)  
2) A specific project hook (location + designer + year)  
3) 3 proof bullets (speed %, cost %, risk avoided)  
4) A designer takeaway section (transferable principles)  
5) CTA (download / sample / contact)
> **中文：** 可复用结构：大标题 → 项目钩子（地点/设计师/年份）→ 3 个证据点 → “给设计师的可迁移结论” → CTA（下载/样品/咨询）。

## 5.3 Email tone (brand in 1:1) / 邮件语气（品牌的 1:1 表现）
**EN:**
- Warm, respectful, specific (reference the meeting, their projects, their strengths).
- Avoid hard sell; invite collaboration (lunch presentation, early engagement, prototypes).
- Always include the “what we bring” 5-bullet list and a low-friction next step.
> **中文：**  
> - 温暖、尊重、具体（提到对方项目与优势）。  
> - 不硬推，强调协作（午餐分享、早期介入、打样）。  
> - 固定带“我们能带来什么”5 点清单 + 一个低摩擦下一步。

## 5.4 Copy blocks (approved building blocks) / 常用文案模块（推荐复用）
**EN blocks:**
- “Design-led stone solutions for streetscapes & civil landscapes.”
- “Shop drawings + off-site pre-assembly = predictable outcomes.”
- “From sketch to specification to install—WE BUILD. YOU DESIGN.”
- “Carbon-neutral options with full life-cycle offsets.”
> **中文：**  
> - “面向街景与市政景观的设计主导石材解决方案。”  
> - “深化图 + 预装配 = 可预期交付。”  
> - “从手绘到规范到安装——你设计，我建造。”  
> - “全生命周期碳抵消的碳中和选项。”

## 5.5 Standard “what we bring” list (sales-safe) / 标准“我们能带来什么”（可用于销售与官网）
**EN (keep as a consistent block):**
- **End-to-end collaboration:** early design input → documentation → installation support  
- **Material range:** granite, bluestone, sandstone + bespoke finishes  
- **Local + offshore capacity:** Melbourne base + overseas partners for complex geometry  
- **Sustainability & responsibility:** carbon-neutral options, responsible sourcing, emerging Australian quarry partnerships  
- **Technical & logistics support:** shop drawings, pre-assembly, coordination, installation/maintenance guidance  
> **中文（建议固定复用）：**  
> - **端到端协作：** 早期设计输入→文档深化→安装支持  
> - **材料范围：** 花岗岩、玄武岩、砂岩 + 定制表面  
> - **本地+海外产能：** 墨尔本据点 + 海外伙伴支持复杂几何  
> - **可持续与责任：** 碳中和选项、负责任采购、探索澳洲本地采石场合作  
> - **技术与物流支持：** 深化图、预装配、项目协调、安装/维护指导

# 8) Content modules & data schema / 内容模块与数据字段（给内容/产品/设计落地）

## 8.1 Home (must-have modules) / 首页（必备模块）
**EN modules:**
- Hero (tagline + 1 proof hook + CTA)
- Carbon-neutral commitment (scope + method + proof + download)
- Workflow (3-step install + typical timeline)
- Capabilities (Sketch → Budget → Engineering → Shop drawings → Pre-assembly → Manufacture → Install)
- Featured projects (cards + filters)
- Featured materials/finishes (stone library entry)
- Sample request / contact strip
> **中文：** Hero、碳中和承诺、三步流程、能力链路、精选案例、材料/表面入口、样品/咨询收口。

## 8.2 Project page template / 案例页模板
**EN required fields:**
- Project name, location, year, client type
- Designer, contractor
- Stone(s), finish(es), quantity (LM / units), carbon offset (Yes/No), lead time / installation time (if safe to disclose)
- The “why” (design intent) + the “how” (engineering + pre-assembly) + proof (photos/data)
> **中文：** 必填字段：项目名/地点/年份/客户类型、设计师/承包商、石材/表面/体量、是否碳抵消、周期（可披露则写）、设计意图 + 工程交付 + 证据。

## 8.3 Material / stone library template / 材料与石材库模板
**EN required fields:**
- Stone name, stone type, color tone, recommended applications
- Finish list (each finish needs high-res + behavior notes: slip, glare, maintenance)
- Availability (local/offshore), sample options, downloads
> **中文：** 必填：石材名/类型/色调/适用场景；表面清单（每个表面都要高精图 + 行为说明）；供货与样品/下载。

## 8.4 Concrete vs Stone (decision hub) / Concrete vs Stone（决策中心）
**EN sections:**
- Process comparison (7-step concrete vs 3-step stone)
- “Surprises” list + Urblo prevention system (shop drawings, pre-assembly, streamline install)
- Total cost breakdown logic (with assumptions)
- Longevity & maintenance (efflorescence, cracking, fading vs UV-stable stone, pre-seal)
> **中文：** 流程对比、现场“惊喜”清单与预防体系、全成本逻辑（含假设）、耐久与维护对比。

## 8.5 Content franchises (repeatable editorial system) / 内容栏目（可持续的内容系统）
**EN:** Use repeatable series so a small team can publish consistently, and every piece pushes a decision forward.  
**Recommended series (from newsletters):**
- **Project Spotlight** — one project, one problem, one measurable win.
- **Behind the Scenes** — pre-assembly, QC checks, tooling, logistics (build trust).
- **Material Mastery** — one stone, many finishes (“one stone, eight personalities”).
- **Concrete vs Stone** — myth-busting with assumptions and evidence.
- **Details that matter** — LED grooves, conduit drilling, curves, tolerances (“precision in the dark”).
> **中文：** 用“可复用栏目”降低团队内容负担，每篇内容都要推进决策。推荐栏目：项目聚焦、幕后工艺、材料工艺课、混凝土 vs 石材辟谣、关键细节（灯槽/走线/曲线/容差）。

## 8.6 Product & naming architecture (how to present products) / 产品与命名架构（产品如何呈现）
**EN principles:**
- Present products as **systems/series**, not single SKUs.
- Each series page must include: what it is → where it fits → modular rules → finish options → install workflow → downloads.
- Hybrid narratives are allowed (stone + timber/steel/light) as long as buildability is shown.
**Example (already used in newsletters):**
- **PrimeBlock Series** (clean square profile; modular flexibility; LED-ready; material harmony)
- **PrimeBlock Core** (standardized hero product)
> **中文：** 原则：用“系列/系统”表达，不要像零散 SKU；每个系列页都要包含定位、适用场景、模块规则、表面选项、安装流程、下载；允许石+木/钢/灯光的混合叙事，但必须证明可施工。例：PrimeBlock Series / PrimeBlock Core。

## 9.3 Claim library & usage rules / 常用“数据口径”与使用规则
**EN (examples seen across materials):**
